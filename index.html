THE ORIGIN STORY

From Firefighter to
Firestarter of Human Potential

George Dans is fired up with passion and drive to help you develop as he focuses on ways to improve your life by understanding the power of setting and reaching your goals.

Honored as Academy Chief at the fire academy, George served our country as a firefighter before channeling that same relentless energy into transforming how people sell, lead, and live.

He has spoken at worldwide conventions with over 30,000 people in attendance. His approach is dynamic, exciting, and interactive. He is a master of humor and powerful storytelling, bringing unparalleled energy and principles to every audience.

George is a leading authority on the development of human potential, personal and professional effectiveness, and business leadership. He has shared the stage with General Colin Powell, Frank Abagnale, Rick Wagner, and Mike Singletary.

THE TORCH METHOD

The C.A.S.H. Philosophy

C

Commitment

Your attitude is the spark. Quit aiming for average, because you will hit it with amazing accuracy.

S

Skills

Learning a new skill can be taught and used today. George proves it by getting audiences to say yes over 100 times in 7 minutes.

H

Habits

Be process driven, not commission driven. Build the daily habits that make peak performance automatic.
